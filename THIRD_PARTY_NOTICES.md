# Third-Party Notices

ImagePress bundles command-line encoders and runtime libraries so release downloads work without Homebrew or other external installs.

Bundled components:

- `cwebp` and WebP libraries from WebP
- `avifenc` and libavif
- AOM
- dav1d
- libpng
- libjpeg-turbo
- libtiff
- zstd
- xz/liblzma
- libvmaf

Their license files are copied into the app bundle at:

```text
ImagePress.app/Contents/Resources/ThirdPartyLicenses/
```

ImagePress itself is licensed under GPL-3.0. See `LICENSE`.
